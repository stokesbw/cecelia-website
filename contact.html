A Note on Correspondence

I welcome inquiries related to my research, teaching, or public scholarship. Whether you are a fellow scholar, a prospective collaborator, a journalist or media producer, or someone with questions about my work, please feel free to reach out by email.

I respond to most correspondence within a few business days. For time-sensitive inquiries, including academic application materials, please note that in the subject line.

I am currently on the academic job market and am available for discussions of tenure-track positions, postdoctoral fellowships, and lecturer or visiting faculty roles.

Podcast & Media Inquiries

For podcast appearances, media interviews, or public-facing engagements related to Latinx literature and culture, Marvel and superhero media, or public humanities, please contact by email and indicate the nature of the inquiry.
